Major Career Records

  • Fastest ODI century: 100 in 31 balls (against West Indies in 2015)
  • Fastest 50 in ODIs: 50 in 16 balls (against West Indies in 2015)
  • Fastest 150 in ODIs: 150 in 64 balls (against West Indies in 2015)
  • Only player to average over 50 with a strike rate above 100 in ODIs
  • Third-most sixes in ODI history: 204

Notable Achievements

  • ICC ODI Player of the Year three times: 2010, 2014, 2015
  • Captain of South Africa in all formats between 2012 and 2017
  • Scored two 200+ partnerships in ODIs with Hashim Amla
  • Holds the record for most innings without a duck in Test cricket (78 innings)
  • Most sixes in a Test innings for South Africa: 16

IPL Records

  • Played for Delhi Daredevils (2008–2010) and Royal Challengers Bangalore (2011–2021)
  • Scored 5,162 runs in IPL at an average of 39.71 and a strike rate of 151.69
  • Third-most sixes in IPL history: 251
  • Three IPL centuries (highest score: 133* vs Mumbai Indians in 2015)
  • Only player to score 75+ runs and take 3+ catches in the same IPL match (vs MI, 2015)
